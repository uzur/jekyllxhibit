This archive is now closed

From 2014-2015, I maintained an online archive of all the work that I made. I had published everything that I had made, even the stuff that wasn't that great, and licensed most of it under a Creative Commons Attribution license (and some CC0).

I have decided to close this site as I had not foreseen the extent to which it would become indexed by search engines. For the respect of those that appear in many of my photographs and their privacy, I have scrubbed all of the content from this site.

Sincerely,

Tom Hackshaw
